Payslips

Payslips module enables you to view your Payslips.

How do I Upload Payslips?

  1. Click Payslips in the top menu

  2. Click Upload Payslips in the left menu
  3. Click +Add button on the gridâ€™s top right corner

  4. Enter the required details
  5. Click Save button to upload payslips

How do I view/download Uploaded Payslips?

  1. Click Payslips in the top menu

  2. Click Upload Payslips in the left menu
  3. Click on a record to view

  4. Click on .csv file to download and view the uploaded payslips

How do I view/download my Payslips?

  1. Click Payslips in the top menu
  2. Click View Payslips in the left menu
  3. Click on a record to view

  4. Click on .pdf file to download and view the uploaded Payslips
