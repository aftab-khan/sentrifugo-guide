Conference bookings module enables you to book a specific conference room. It displays all the bookings done by employees/users and also their availability at a particular time period.

How to Create Conference Rooms?

  1. Click on Conference Bookings in the top menu.
  2. Click on Manage Conference Rooms in the left menu panel.

  1. Click +Add button available on the top right side

  1. Fill in the required details.
  2. Click SAVE button to create a new conference room

  1. You can Edit/Delete and manage your conference rooms in the Landing page.

The Manage conference roomâ€™s page will not be visible to any other role except super admin.

How to Schedule Bookings for a conference room?

  1. Click on Conference Bookings in the top menu.
  2. Scheduled Bookings page will be displayed by default. A calendar for the current week along with all day timings is displayed here.

  1. Click on the desired Date and time when you would like to book a conference room.
  2. You can also drag the time slot you would like to reserve. This will open a pop up window as shown below.

  1. Fill in the details displayed.
  2. Click on Save to book your time slot for a conference room.
  3. All the bookings made for different conference rooms available will be displayed in the calendar as shown in the image below.
  4. Bookings history can also be viewed by clicking on the All Bookings in the left menu panel.

  • The user cannot book /schedule a conference room for a previous date or time.
  • An email notification will be sent to all the members/participants about the booking.

How to Cancel a Scheduled Booking?

  1. Select the booking you would like to cancel. A pop up window will be displayed as shown below.

  1. Select Cancelled status from the drop down.
  2. Click on Update to cancel the booking made. This will be reflected in the calendar.

An email notification will be sent to all the members/participants of the scheduled booking regarding the cancellation.
