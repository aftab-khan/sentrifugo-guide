How do I view My Details

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(a) Click My Details on the left menu panel

Only the Super Admin/Management/HR has the privilege to add/edit all employees

Official

This option displays all your official details such as Employee ID, Email, and Business Unit etc. You cannot edit/add any detail except your contact number.

Documents

You can upload documents like birth certificate, degree certificate, tax proof documents etc.

leaves

You can view the leave limit, number of used leaves and the leave balance for a particular year.

Click on the record to check the break-up of your leaves

Salery

You can view your salary and account details.

Personal

(a) Click Edit icon to add or modify any details

You can add or edit details for other submenu options like Contact, Skills, Job History, and Education etc.

The submenu options appear depending on the Employee Configuration options selected by the Super Admin/Management/HR. Please refer section

How do I view My Team details

This option will appear only for users who have employees reporting to them.

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(b) Click My Team on the left menu panel

(c) Click +Add button to add an employee to your team (Refer section 1.4.2 Adding Other Employees)

(or)

Click Edit icon to view or edit employee details.

How do I add my Skills

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(b) Click My Skills in the left menu

(c) Click on +Add button on the grid’s top right corner

(d) Enter the Required details

(e) Click SAVE button

How do I edit/update my Skills?

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(b) Click My Skills in the left menu

(c) Click on Edit icon in the action column

(d) Enter or modify details

(e) Click UPDATE button

How do I view my Skills?

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(b) Click My Skills in the left menu

Click on the record to view the details.

How do I delete my Skills?

(a) Click Self Service in the top menu

(b) Click My Skills in the left menu

(c) Click on Delete icon in the action column

(d) Click Yes to delete a skill
