Recruitments

Recruitments simplifies your hiring process by giving you the provision to initialize a requisition, manage candidate CVs, interviews and shortlist/select candidates. Below is the recruitment process flowchart.

Description:

  • A User (Management/Manager/HR) raises a requisition request. According to the number of approvers selected (Min: 1 Max: 3), the approvers (Management, Manager) have to approve/reject the requisition request. A requisition can be rejected at any level
  • After the requisition request has been approved, the HR can enter the details (CV) of the candidate
  • The HR will then schedule an interview
  • The interview takes place offline
  • The interviewer (Management/Manager/HR/Employee) provides the feedback about the candidate
  • The Management has to give their final consent, they can either approve/reject
  • Once the Management approves, the HR can add the candidate to the application

How do I create a Job Requisition/Opening?

  1. Click Recruitments in the top menu
  2. Click Openings/Positions on the left menu panel
  3. Click +Add button on the right side


  4. Requisition ID will be generated automatically
  5. Enter the required details
  6. Select the approver(s)
  7. Click SAVE button

How do I Approve/Reject a Requisition/Opening?

  1. Click Recruitments in the top menu
  2. Click Openings/Positions on the left menu panel
  3. Click Edit icon in the action column

  4. Select an action (Approve/Reject) in the field requisition status
  5. Click UPDATE button

How do I enter a Candidate’s details?

  1. Click Recruitments in the top menu
  2. Click Candidates on the left side panel
  3. Click +Add button on the right side

  4. Select the Requisition ID
  5. Enter Candidate’s name f1. Click to upload resume

    6. or

  6. f2. Click here to enter candidate details in a form
  7. Click SAVE button

How do I Schedule an Interview?

  1. Click Recruitments in the top menu
  2. Click Interviews on the left menu panel
  3. Click +Add button on the right side

  4. Select the Requisition ID
  5. Add candidate details if they aren’t added
  6. Enter the required details
  7. Click SAVE button

The interviewer and candidate will receive email notifications informing them about the interview.

How do I provide Feedback for an Interview?

  1. Click Recruitments menu option
  2. Click Interviews on the left side panel
  3. Click Edit icon against an interview

  4. Click Edit icon against an interview round

  5. Provide feedback, comments and select the round status
  6. Click UPDATE button

  7. Click +Add to add another interview round (Provided you have given ‘Schedule for next round’ as round status)
  8. Provide the interview and candidate status
  9. Click UPDATE button

How do I select/reject a Shortlisted Candidate? (Management’s Final Approval)

  1. Click Recruitments in the top menu
  2. Click Shortlisted & Selected Candidates on the left menu panel
  3. Click Edit icon against a requisition code

  4. Select the status (select/reject)
  5. Click UPDATE button

How do I add a Candidate as an Employee?

  1. Click HR in the top menu
  2. Click+Add button on the right side

  3. Select Interview in the field Mode of Employment
  4. The shortlisted candidates’ names will be populated here, select a candidate from the drop down list
  5. Enter all the required details
  6. Click SAVE button

An activity log will be displayed for every action performed by user(s) involved in a Recruitment Cycle. This will provide users more clarity about the status of the process.
